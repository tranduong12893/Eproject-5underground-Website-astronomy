Welcome to
Infinite Universe

“INFINITE UNIVERSE” is a project which builds nonprofit websites for communities who pay a great passion for astronomy, astrophysics and space technologies.

Purpose

The foundation of “INFINITE UNIVERSE”” is to supply overall information about universe and articles relating to astronomic – world universe activities and events. The website also brings about interesting and useful information for the communities and helps lovers for astronomy, astrophysics and space technologies to access specialized scientific activities, which can become a bridge for the lovers and realistic scientific activities.

Precept

“INFINITE UNIVERSE” is founded with the slogan that “Share our passion”. Being big enthusiasts about astronomy, astrophysics and space technologies, “INFINITE UNIVERSE” Admin Team will spread our inspiration out the communities through high quality articles, update and helpful information. “INFINITE UNIVERSE” is also a connector among people who share the same passion. With such wonderful meanings, “INFINITE UNIVERSE” is expected to develop and become a loyal partner of all lovers for astronomy, astrophysics and space technologies.
