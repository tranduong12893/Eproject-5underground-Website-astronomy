Redshift software overview Explore the universe, astronomy

Explore the universe from your PC with professional and award-winning astrophotography software. Redshift is your comprehensive guide to the heavens. Enjoy a dazzling, breathtaking representation of the night sky; Travel across the Milky Way and beyond, or get up close and detailed views of planets, moons, asteroids or many other celestial bodies. Use Redshift's powerful telescopic controls every time you observe the night sky. With comprehensive, customizable Sky Diary support, you can easily plan any of your own observations.

New features of Redshift 8 Premium

  • 100 million stars, 1 million deep sky objects and 500,000 asteroids
  • Extended interval (4713 BC - 9999 AD)
  • Integrated number of motion of asteroids and comets
  • Land / Take off from planet and moon surface
  • Map of eclipse on the globe 3D Earth
  • 3D flights to planets and moons, asteroids, spaceships, stars, clusters and galaxies
  • 40 interactive multimedia tours, including breathtaking photos, videos and animations
