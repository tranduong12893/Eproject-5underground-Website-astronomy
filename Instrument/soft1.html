Explore the 3D galaxy with Celestia

There are many free space exploration programs out there on the Internet, but Celestia.space is one of the best. Celestia not only lets you explore the Milky Way on your own, through stunning animations, but also lets you control time and see how objects interact. Whether you want to track an eclipse on the surface of a planet or watch the orbit of a comet, you have complete control over everything.
All planets and stars in Celestia are displayed in full 3D. You can zoom and rotate them with seamless animations. Celestia is truly gorgeous and completely free. Here's how you can get started exploring the app.

Unlike most astronomical software, Celestia does not limit you to only exploring the Earth's surface. You can travel around the solar system, visit one of more than 100,000 stars, even go deep into the galaxy.

All moves in Celestia are seamless. The exponential zoom allows you to explore a vast expanse of space, from clusters of galaxies to spacecraft just a few meters apart. The "touch and go" interface makes it easy for users to navigate the universe to the objects they want to visit.

3D universe simulation

Celestia simulates many different types of celestial bodies. From planets, moons to star clusters and galaxies, you can visit individual objects in Celestia's extensive database, viewing it at any point in space and time. The position and motion of objects in the solar system are accurately calculated in real time at any scale the user wants.

Interactive Observatory

You can easily navigate to any world & land on its surface. When using Celestia as the observatory, the software accurately displays the positions of objects in the solar system. You can enable / disable symbols and other assist features using the shortcut key or zoom of the desired object.

Open content

Celestia allows users to easily expand the portfolio with many available add-ons, containing new objects such as comets, constellations, high-quality Earth images, other solar system parts along with celestial models 3D jelly, spaceship in precise orbit. Even fictional objects from famous movies can be found.

Create your own world

If you cannot find the object you need in the list, you can create it yourself. Build entire system of planets, nebulae, galaxies and fictional objects. Celestia offers you a unique opportunity to interact with your own creative products.
